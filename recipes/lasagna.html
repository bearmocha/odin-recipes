How to Make Lasagna

This recipe will teach you how to make lasagna with just a few simple ingredients! Enjoy a hearty lasagna with layers of cheese and beef.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Seasonings
  • Water
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef
  2. Stir in spaghetti sauce and let simmer
  3. Mix the cheeses with eggs and seasonings
  4. Layer the beef, cheese and noodles
  5. Cover the lasagna with foil and bake for 45 minutes
  6. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
